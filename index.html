Dodge The Knives

A game where you Dodge the Knives! So basically you are the dragon and you have to hold [SPACE] to jump. Collect coins, and post your highest. 5 hits from knives can kill you.

Cascading Bombs Annihilation

"Cascading Bombs Annihilation" is a multiplayer battle game in which you collect food to gain coins and health. The zombies take away health and coins. Grey movement: the arrows, Purple movement: 8 (jump), 4 (left), 6 (right), and Orange movement: W(jump), A(left), D(right)
